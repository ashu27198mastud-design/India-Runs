# RANKFORGE AI — Intelligent Candidate Discovery and Ranking Platform

## Slide 1: Title Slide
* **Product name:** RANKFORGE AI
* **Tagline:** Beyond filtering. Evidence-backed candidate intelligence.
* **Challenge:** Intelligent Candidate Discovery
* **One-line pitch:** "RANKFORGE AI transforms job descriptions, resumes, projects, career metadata, and activity signals into an explainable, bias-aware, intelligently ranked shortlist of candidates."

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
Why traditional ATS systems fail:
* They over-rely on keywords.
* They miss hidden gems.
* They rank keyword-stuffed resumes too high.
* They do not understand context.
* They do not explain ranking decisions.
* They do not integrate career trajectory, project evidence, or activity signals.

## Slide 3: Proposed Solution
RANKFORGE AI Core Features:
* Deep job understanding engine
* Candidate DNA engine
* Semantic fit engine
* Multi-signal ranking engine
* Hidden Gem Detector
* Overfit Detector
* Explainable ranking
* Fairness firewall
* Hiring manager brief generator

## Slide 5: Ranking Methodology
Scoring dimensions:
* Mandatory skill match
* Semantic skill adjacency
* Domain relevance
* Experience depth
* Project evidence
* Career trajectory
* Activity signals
* Availability fit
* Risk adjustment
* Evidence confidence

*The system does not produce a black-box score. Each candidate rank is supported by evidence, confidence, risk indicators, and explanation.*

## Slide 6: Differentiators
* Hidden Gem Detector
* Overfit Detector
* Evidence Graph
* Candidate DNA
* Job DNA
* Configurable scoring weights
* Explainable ranking
* Fairness firewall

## Slide 7: Responsible AI and Governance
* AI does not make final hiring decisions.
* Protected attributes are excluded.
* Ranking is based only on job-relevant evidence.
* Human review is mandatory.
* Scoring is explainable.
* Audit trail is maintained.
* Fairness warnings are shown.

## Slide 8: Demo Flow
1. Recruiter enters job description.
2. System generates Job DNA.
3. System ranks candidates.
4. User opens "Why this rank?"
5. User views Hidden Gem Detector.
6. User views Overfit Detector.
7. User compares top candidates.
8. User exports ranked output and hiring brief.
